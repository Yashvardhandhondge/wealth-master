Understanding Crypto Staking

Staking is the process of actively participating in transaction validation (similar to mining) on a proof-of-stake (PoS) blockchain. On these blockchains, anyone with a minimum-required balance of a specific cryptocurrency can validate transactions and earn staking rewards.

Exploring Crypto Lending

Crypto lending is a form of decentralized finance (DeFi) where investors lend their cryptocurrencies to borrowers in exchange for interest payments. It's a way to make your assets work for you.

  • High potential returns
  • Contributes to network security
  • Relatively easy to get started
